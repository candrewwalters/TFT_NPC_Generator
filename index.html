Thanks for trying out this modest NPC generator for The Fantasy Trip! Press a button and get a character in the standard format, suitable for importing into the TFT Helper mobile app, Henry Cobb's TFT CP Calculator, and other fine software that uses the official format. You can either choose a type of character and a race, or choose a context and these will be chosen for you.

The aim here is to produce some typical folks, not exotic people decked out in adventure hooks. They're fleshed out to be more than wallpaper, but they aren't principle characters. And that means no wizards, no warriors, just casual encounters.

Needless to say, this could go a lot further, and maybe it will.

You could even gussy this up yourself. The javascript here is, let's call it idiosyncratic, but it should be easy enough to follow. Particularly the charater types near the top. You will instantly see how they are laid out, and you can add any number. Follow the format and they will appear in the Type menu and work. Similarly, you can just add names to the list at the bottom and they will be included in the selection. You can't hurt anything by trying.
